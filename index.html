Who We Are

Your Guide to the
Philippine Wild

TrailPH is a passion project born from a love of the outdoors and a deep pride in the Philippines' extraordinary natural landscapes. From the misty peaks of the Cordillera to the volcanic shores of Bicol, our mission is to connect hikers — local and foreign alike — with the trails that define this archipelago.

Every route in our collection is personally scouted, verified, and curated with safety, sustainability, and cultural respect in mind. We partner with local guides, DENR-registered groups, and indigenous communities to ensure each adventure enriches rather than diminishes the landscapes we celebrate.
